AI Minecraft Mobs Encyclopedia

Mobs

Zombie

Zombies are common hostile mobs that attack players at night and can wear armor.

Creeper

Creepers are explosive hostile mobs silently roaming the land, capable of causing significant damage to players and structures.

Enderman

Endermen are tall, neutral mobs that can teleport and pick up blocks. They become hostile when looked at directly or attacked.

Spider

Spiders are agile hostile mobs that can climb walls and are not affected by poison. They become neutral to the player in daylight.

Ender Dragon

The Ender Dragon is a formidable boss mob that guards the End. Defeating it opens the gateway to return to the Overworld and spawns an egg.

Cat

Cats are tameable passive mobs that can scare away creepers and give gifts to their owners.

Dog (Wolf)

Wolves can be tamed with bones and turned into dogs that follow and protect their owner.

Pig

Pigs are passive mobs that can be ridden with a saddle and controlled with a carrot on a stick.

Cow

Cows are passive mobs that can be milked and provide leather and beef when slaughtered.

Axolotl

Axolotls are aquatic passive mobs that can assist players in underwater combat.

Horse

Horses are tameable mobs that can be ridden to travel faster over land.

Panda

Pandas are passive mobs found in jungles that exhibit various personalities and can be fed bamboo.

Villager

Villagers are passive mobs that inhabit villages, work at their professions, and trade with players.
